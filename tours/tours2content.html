Luxembourg

Luxembourg, officially known as the Grand Duchy of Luxembourg, is a small, landlocked country in Western Europe, bordered by Belgium, France, and Germany. Despite its size, Luxembourg is renowned for its rich history, stunning landscapes, and status as one of the world's wealthiest countries. The country blends modernity with tradition, offering a dynamic mix of medieval castles, world-class museums, and picturesque countryside.
